A project connecting long-time and new residents of the neighborhood through a series of community meals hosted at the garden. Participants were invited to share what they knew — recipes, stories, histories — and to meet those who had arrived after the neighborhood changed.

The meals were designed as facilitated conversations using the garden itself as a container: a place where things are planted and return season after season.
